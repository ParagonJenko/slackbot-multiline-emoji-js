**IdeaSquared — BigMoji Generator**

We've written this in plain English on purpose. You deserve to actually understand what happens with your data — not wade through legal boilerplate. That said, this policy does meet the requirements of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR, Articles 13 & 14).

## The short version

- 🖼️ **Your images never leave your device.** All processing happens in your browser.
- 📊 **We collect anonymous usage analytics** via Segment so we can improve the tool.
- 🚫 **We don't collect your name, email, or any directly identifying information.**
- 🍪 **No advertising. No selling your data. Ever.**

## 1. Your images stay on your device

When you upload an image or GIF, it is processed entirely inside your browser using the Canvas API. Nothing you upload is sent to our servers or any third party. Once you close or refresh the page, your images are gone — we never see them.

---

## 2. Anonymous analytics (Segment)

To understand how people use BigMoji and make it better, we use **Segment** (Twilio Inc., 101 Spear Street, San Francisco, CA 94105, USA) to collect anonymous usage data.

Here's what gets collected:

- Pages visited and actions taken (e.g. "file processed")
- The filename you processed (just the name — the file itself stays on your device)
- Browser type and version
- Approximate location derived from your IP address (country/region level)

Segment assigns a random anonymous identifier to your browser session and stores it in your browser's local storage. This identifier contains no personal information — it's just a random string so we can distinguish unique visits.

Your data is processed on Segment's servers in the United States under Standard Contractual Clauses, which provide an appropriate level of protection for transfers outside the EU.

**Legal basis:** Article 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interest in understanding how the tool is used so we can improve it.

**To opt out:** Use a content blocker such as [uBlock Origin](https://ublockorigin.com) or disable third-party scripts in your browser settings.

## 3. Server logs

BigMoji is hosted as a static site. Like any website, the hosting infrastructure automatically records standard server log data when you visit, including your IP address, browser type, and the pages you requested. These logs are used solely for security and infrastructure purposes and are not linked to any personal profile.

---

## 4. Data retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained by Segment in accordance with their data retention policy (typically 12 months). Server logs are retained for a short period for security purposes. We don't store any personal data beyond this.

## 5. Your rights under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding any personal data we hold about you:

- **Access** — ask us what data we hold about you
- **Rectification** — ask us to correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction** — ask us to limit how we process your data
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Object** — object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw consent** — if processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, just email us at [help@ideasquared.co.uk](mailto:help@ideasquared.co.uk) and we'll respond promptly.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. You can find your local authority at [this link](https://www.bfdi.bund.de/DE/Service/Anschriften/Laender/Laender-node.html).

## 9. Legal definitions (GDPR reference)

If you want the formal definitions that underpin this policy, here they are:

- **Personal data** — any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
- **Processing** — any operation performed on personal data (collection, storage, use, deletion, etc.).
- **Controller** — the entity that determines how and why personal data is processed. That's us.
- **Processor** — a third party that processes data on behalf of the controller. That's Segment.
- **Consent** — a freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous indication of agreement.
- **Legitimate interest** — a lawful basis for processing where our interest in doing so is not overridden by your rights and freedoms.
