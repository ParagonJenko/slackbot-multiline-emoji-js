Image Splitter

A small tool to split images into square tiles, like large Slack emojis (bigmojis).

Preview:

How do I get my own bigmojis:

  1. Upload your emoji - this has to be an image!
  2. Choose how many squares horizontally you want it to be - this can be no more than 10!
  3. Optionally, insert a name for your emoji
  4. Hit Split!
  5. Download the emojis.
  6. Upload these to your Slack workspace.
  7. Insert the command from the textbox to Slackbot.
  8. Profit ???
