BigMoji Generator

A small tool to split images into square tiles, like large Slack emojis (bigmojis).

Preview:

Want to create your very own Bigmojis? It's easy:

  1. Upload your emoji as an image (a square image works best just click this link!).
  2. Select how many squares you want horizontally (up to 10).
  3. (Optional) Give your emoji a name.
  4. Hit the Split button!
  5. Click the 'Split' button.
  6. Download your Bigmojis using the 'Download' button.
  7. Add them to your Slack workspace. You can do this in your Slack app under 'Settings' -> 'Customize,' or visit {workspace}.slack.com/customize/emoji.
  8. Insert the provided command into Slackbot. You can access Slackbot settings in your Slack app under 'Settings' -> 'Customize' or visit {workspace}.slack.com/customize/slackbot.
  9. Enjoy sharing and communicating with your awesome Bigmojis!
